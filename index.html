Know About Us

Technology is a hot sector and its exciting to see more women getting involved . There is an important market for technical and non-technical skills.

JosTechGirls is an initiative to create a level playing field for women by growing the female tech community starting from Jos, It is about growing Women, Gender Balance and Innovation in Technology.

Building a girls tech community in jos, will increase the engagement and gender balance would grow to a reasonable number. This platform is therefore for every woman in jos irrespective of the technology background and social status .
